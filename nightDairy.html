The Night Diary

“The Night Diary” by Veera Hiranandani is a poignant and thought-provoking historical novel set in 1947 during the Partition of India. Told through the eyes of 12-year-old Nisha in the form of letters to her deceased mother, the story captures the struggles of a family forced to flee their home due to religious and political divisions. This moving narrative explores themes of identity, belonging, and courage, making it a must-read for fans of heartfelt and reflective storytelling
